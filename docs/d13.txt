# D13 – DNS Failure Scenario

## Objective
Recreate a DNS issue, investigate it with command-line tools, and capture DNS traffic for evidence.

## Commands
```powershell
nslookup example.com
Test-NetConnection <dns> -Port 53

## Key Results

- Recreated a DNS-related failure scenario in the lab
- Used `nslookup` and `Test-NetConnection` to investigate the issue
- Captured DNS traffic in Wireshark for evidence
- Wrote a runbook with validation and rollback steps
- Confirmed that the issue could be reproduced, analyzed, and reversed

## What I Learned

A good troubleshooting lab should include both the failure state and the recovery process.  
This exercise helped me practice structured troubleshooting instead of jumping directly to a fix.
