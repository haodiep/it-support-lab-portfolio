## Key Results

- Practiced using display filters in Wireshark

- Collected screenshot evidence showing filtered traffic views

## What I Learned

Display filters do not change the capture itself; they only change how captured traffic is viewed.  
This helped me clearly separate traffic collection from traffic analysis.
